One night, your  village of Lunaris is attacked. Amidst the chaos, you barely escape with your life. In the ruins, you discover an ancient scroll. As you unroll it, your eyes widen as you read aloud the line that changes everything. "The chosen one, <name>, destined to overthrow the Emperor and restore peace to Eldoria". With your home reduced to ashes and feeling utterly powerless, you resolve to embark on a journey to fulfill the prophecy.
